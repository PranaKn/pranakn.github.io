## 🛡️ About

Welcome to the **Cybersecurity Knowledge Hub**.

This site is a collection of notes, research, and observations about the evolving world of cybersecurity. It focuses primarily on **defensive security**, exploring how attacks happen, how they are detected, and how defenders can better understand the behavior of adversaries.

You will find topics such as:

- Threat hunting
- Malware analysis
- Security monitoring
- Incident response
- Detection engineering
- Cybersecurity research and books
- Daily threat landscape updates

Most of the content comes from **experiments, labs, investigations, and ongoing study**. The goal is simple: document knowledge, refine understanding, and share insights that may help others navigate the same path.

> _Cybersecurity is a continuous process of learning, observing, and adapting._

The field changes constantly, and staying curious is often the best defense.
